Product Description

Our Immune Support Tincture is a potent blend of herbs traditionally used to support the immune system. This powerful formula combines the protective qualities of echinacea, elderberry, and astragalus, along with other supportive herbs.

Each batch is carefully crafted in small quantities to ensure the highest quality and potency. Our alcohol-based extraction method captures the full spectrum of beneficial plant compounds for maximum effectiveness.

Benefits:

  • Supports healthy immune function
  • Helps the body's natural defense mechanisms
  • Provides antioxidant support
  • Promotes overall wellness

Ingredients

Our tinctures are made with the highest quality, sustainably harvested herbs. All ingredients are organic or wildcrafted whenever possible.

  • Echinacea Root (Echinacea purpurea): Traditionally used to support the immune system.
  • Elderberry (Sambucus nigra): Rich in antioxidants and known for immune support.
  • Astragalus Root (Astragalus membranaceus): An adaptogen traditionally used to strengthen vitality.
  • Reishi Mushroom (Ganoderma lucidum): Supports immune function and overall wellness.
  • Ginger Root (Zingiber officinale): Warming herb that supports circulation.
  • Alcohol Base (40%): Organic cane alcohol used for optimal extraction.
  • Distilled Water: Pure water used in the extraction process.

How to Use

Take 30-40 drops (approximately 1 dropperful) up to 3 times daily, or as recommended by your healthcare professional. For best absorption, place under the tongue and hold for 30 seconds before swallowing.

Recommended Usage:

  • For daily immune support: Take 1 dropperful daily.
  • During times of increased need: Take 1 dropperful 3 times daily.

Caution: Consult your healthcare provider before using if pregnant, nursing, taking medication or have a medical condition. Not recommended for children under 12 years of age.
